When I was a student, I worked for a construction company in Minsk (May 2018 – May 2019). Made “cold calls”. Designed some projects on AutoCAD. I was responsible for bills of materials and commercial offers, as well as ordering processing and materials delivery (I have a driving license cat. B). Worked in different cities such as Minsk, Orsha, Gomel, Mazyr. I installed and performed maintenance on ventilation and air-conditioning systems. Among other things, I also worked as a low-rank manager of a small team whose work was to install those systems.

I had been working in gas supply company in Vileika after university (July 2019 – July 2021). My functions included in-house gas equipment maintenance, making sure users of such equipment follow the safety regulations concerning handling it, as well as controlling the team of 8 maintenance workers whose responsibility was to look after the gas delivery systems of single-, multi-flat buildings, gas heaters (both wall-mounted and floor-mounted), gas boilers, gas meters, stoves, pressure regulators. I also set timetables for the team and made sure those were strictly followed.
